Areas of Expertise

  • Development of novel drug delivery systems (DDS) based on new carrier materials e.g., naturally derived & synthetic polymers, inorganic materials, hydrogels, nanoparticulate systems​
  • Formulation of nanomedicines based on small and large bioactive molecules (small molecule drugs, peptides, proteins, nucleic acids)
  • Rational DDS design through physico-chemical characterization linking material attributes to processing and performance (including cellular & bacterial evaluation)
